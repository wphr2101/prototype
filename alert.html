Alerts in the Signal Hub application used a methodological decision analysis in four aspects including decision problems, decision makers, decision environments, and decision approaches.

To examine the right resolution for the alert w.r.t the criticality, response time, resolution time, etc. the alert techniques are working from three perspectives: (1) Multi-criteria decision making techniques (2) Mathematical programming techniques and (3) Artificial intelligence techniques.

Alerts functionality will use these techniques and analysed the means of integrating these techniques for a right resolution (or) options of resolution based on the alerts.

The Settings feature of Signal Hub makes it easy to create, store, and maintain customised parameters and user preferences. A setting addresses both needs by providing an easy way to store both customised parameters and user preferences on the Signal Hub.

Settings have no built-in facility for encrypting information automatically. You should never store security-related information, such as database passwords, in clear text. If you want to store such sensitive information, based on the request it will be customised to make sure it is secure.
