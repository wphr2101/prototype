Data functionality is the initial phase of the Signal Hub application starting process. Data process life cycle functionality is to do the collected data are verified and validated. The verification and validation process ensures that the data were collected according to specifications given in the signal hub application engine and that they are appropriate and consistent with their intended use.

Data validation follows the data verification process and uses information from the previous process to ascertain the usability of the data in light of their pre-determined measurement quality objectives and to ensure that results obtained are scientifically defensible.

The Settings feature of Signal Hub makes it easy to create, store, and maintain customised parameters and user preferences. A setting addresses both needs by providing an easy way to store both customised parameters and user preferences on the Signal Hub.

Settings have no built-in facility for encrypting information automatically. You should never store security-related information, such as database passwords, in clear text. If you want to store such sensitive information, based on the request it will be customised to make sure it is secure.
